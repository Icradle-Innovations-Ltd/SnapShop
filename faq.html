Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help customers buy with confidence.

This page reduces uncertainty and saves customer support time.

How long does delivery take?

Kampala deliveries take the same day or next day. Upcountry deliveries usually take one to three working days depending on location.

Which payment methods are supported?

Customers can pay using mobile money, card payment, or cash on delivery for selected areas and order sizes.

Can I change the contents of my shopping cart?

Yes. The cart page allows you to increase quantity, reduce quantity, remove a product, or clear the full cart before checkout.

Do product prices include delivery?

Product prices are shown clearly on the shop page. Delivery is calculated separately at checkout based on the delivery option selected.

How do I contact SnapShop?

You can call, send email, or use the contact form on the Contact page. All major contact details are listed there.
